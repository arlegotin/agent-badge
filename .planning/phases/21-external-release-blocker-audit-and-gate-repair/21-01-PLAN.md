<threat_model>
## Threat Model

### Assets
- truthful production-readiness evidence
- maintainer release decision quality
- credential privacy in saved artifacts

### Threats
- **High:** a false-positive readiness claim caused by relying on local green checks while ignoring live external blockers
- **High:** accidental leakage of auth or credential material into phase artifacts
- **Medium:** ambiguous artifact wording that hides whether ownership or trusted-publisher checks are still unresolved

### Required mitigations
- Save only machine-readable status, package metadata, and operator conclusions; never save tokens or secret values.
- Keep the readiness summary explicit about what is proven locally, what is blocked, and what is still manual.
- Treat any unresolved ownership or trusted-publisher confirmation as unresolved, not implicitly safe.
</threat_model>
