<objective>
Make pre-push automation policy explicit and make degraded hook behavior impossible to confuse with a healthy live badge update.

Purpose: The repo already stores `refresh.prePush.mode`, but the managed hook still hides policy in shell details and hook-mode refresh output is too easy to read as success when fail-soft is active. This plan makes the selected policy explicit in wiring and CLI parsing, then makes degraded hook output loud enough that stale badge risk is obvious.
Output: explicit `--hook-policy` hook plumbing, inspectable pre-push policy output, loud degraded fail-soft messaging, and regression coverage that proves developers cannot mistake a stale badge for success.
</objective>
