# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, technically grounded collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- discuss ideas and bugs in good faith
- focus criticism on code, behavior, and evidence
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, and doxxing
- avoid posting private credentials, transcript content, or sensitive local data

## Unacceptable Behavior

- abusive or discriminatory language
- targeted harassment
- publishing someone else's secrets or private work artifacts
- repeated low-signal disruption after moderation requests

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this policy and may restrict participation when necessary to keep the project usable.
